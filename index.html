Digitising construction

Whilst we design digitally we still construct manually, using the same technology as 100 years ago. Architects can design to a high precision, but once the construction process starts, it becomes completely manual. Itâ€™s this disconnect between digital design and physical construction that causes costly errors, inefficiencies and poor quality buildings that plague the industry.

Our mission at Scaled Robotics is to digitise construction through the introduction of mobile robots to the construction site. These teams of small robots are a physical presence on the site, working directly from the 3D model, bridging the gap between the digital world and physical job site.

Backed by our lead investor and industry partner Peri we are building mobile robots that digitise construction.
